
Brownie Cheesecake! - Keto!
This is from the amazing LCHF UK facebook page.



Ingerdients:
Brownie Base
190g Ground Almonds
55g Cocoa Powder
60g Truvia
57g Melted Butter
57g Sugar Free Maple Syrup
2 large Eggs
13g Psyllium Husk Powder
2 tsp Toffee Flavouring
1 tsp Baking Powder
� tsp Salt

Cheesecake Layer
340g Cream Cheese
100g Truvia
� tsp Vanilla
40g Sour Cream
2 Eggs

Directions:
Lightly grease the sides of a 8" springform tin & line the bottom with greaseproof paper, put onto a baking sheet.
Preheat oven to 175/155c fan.
Prepare the brownie base by mixing all the wet ingredients in one bowl and the dry ingredients in another.
Beat the wet ingredients together using a hand mixer.
Pour the wet ingredients slowly into the dry ingredients, mixing with a hand mixer as you pour.
Press the batter into the prepared springform tin and even the top out.
Put to one side while you prepare the cheesecake layer. 
Beat the cream cheese until fluffy, about 3 minutes. 
Combine with the sweetener, sour cream and vanilla extract, mixing for about another 3 minutes.
Gently add the eggs one at a time. beating until creamy, about one minute after each egg.
Pour in the filling on top of the brownie base. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until puffy and lightly brown around the edges.
Let cool for about 1-2 hours on a wire rack. 
Cover and chill for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight leaving in the tin.
Score around the cold cheesecake and gentle release it from the tin.
Cut into 10 portions and allow to come to room temperature for 10mins.
Serve with whipped cream and a few toasted pecans or a few berries.

Serves 10
Per serving without cream or decoration.
