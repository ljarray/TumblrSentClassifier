
Fresh From Florida Recipe

When I started this blog, one of my goals was to incorporate recipes from the state healthy eating initiative, Fresh From Florida. A big part of healthy eating is to use fresh, local ingredients in your recipes which really set the tone for a well balanced and sustainable diet. When I started this blog, I wanted to feature the recipes this organization creates and show others how easy it can be to eat local. Checkout my recipe for yummy Midsummer Watermelon Salsa, inspired by Fresh From Florida.
