DIRECTIONS

Combine diced mango, apple, bell pepper, onion, garlic, tomatoes and jalapeno in a medium sized mixing bowl. 
Squeeze excess juice from mango skins into bowl. Cut key limes in half and squeeze juice into bowl. Mix to combine.
Add cumin, ginger, dash of salt and pepper. Stir to evenly distribute.Add chopped cilantro and stir.
Cover tightly and chill for at least 2 hours. Stir before serving.
