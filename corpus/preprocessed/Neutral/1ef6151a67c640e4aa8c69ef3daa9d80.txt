
10 Steps Towards SelfLove
I will not let anyone make me feel inferior
I will not base my selfperception off of others perceptions of me
I will not compare my journey or self to others journeys or sense of self
I will continue to strive to love myself
I will surround myself with love and positivity
I will use all negative energy or energy reserved for negative or jealous thoughts to better myself in my eyes
I will not let others define my beauty
I will not let judgements past on me affect who I am
I will not change myself for the sake of those around me
I will not live in fear based on perceived perceptions of myself by others
I am an extremely selfconscious individual Im overly selfaware and constantly questioning my sense of self when Im around other people Its something that I have always battled with and being so has taken a toll on my esteem and chipped away at my public personality I recently decided to take time to sit with myself and discover the root of my feeling this way In doing so Ive pinpointed 10 tipssteps towards selflove and I thought that I would share it with you all in case there are any of you out there doubting yourself because of your selfperceptions through the perceptions of others
